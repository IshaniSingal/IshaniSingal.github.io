About Ishani Singal

Ishani Singal is a 2nd year MBA student at the University of Washigton, Seattle. Pre MBA she has completed her undergrad in Civil Engineering from Manipal Institute of Technology in India followed by her Masters in Structural and earthquake Engineering from University of California, Los Angeles. As part of construction inductry she has taken up the role of both Project manager and Structural Engineer.
During her 4 years in the construction Industry she was involved in the scheduling activities, inventory management, client management both external and internal as well as extensively working with data to analyse the design for high rise buildings. With her knack for data analysis and seeing the need for digital transformation in the construction industry she decided to pursue her MBA.
Post MBA, IShani is looking to join a tech company as a full time product manager. In pursuit to achieve this goal she has taken multiple ML based courses as part of her degree program as well as is currently enrolled in MIT certification to learn coding.
